Avocado Deviled Eggs

This is a twist on the traditional deviled egg. I usually use 1 or 2 fewer yolks for the filling.

Ingredients

  • 6 hard-boiled eggs, peeled and halved
  • 1 avocado - peeled, pitted, and diced
  • 3 slices cooked turkey bacon, chopped, and divided
  • 5/2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 2 teaspoons lime juice
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • sea salt to taste
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, sliced (Optional)
  • 1 dash hot sauce, or to taste (Optional)

Directions

  1. Scoop egg yolks into a bowl; add avocado, 2/3 of chopped turky bacon, mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic, cayenne pepper, and salt. Mash egg yolk mixture until filling is evenly combined.
  2. Spoon filling into a piping bag or plastic bag with a snipped corner. Pip filling into each egg white; top with a turkey bacon piece, jalapeno slice, and a dash of hot sauce.

Nutritional Facts (per serving)

  • 105 calories
  • 4.6g of protein
  • 2.2g of carbohydrates
  • 8.8g of fat
  • 113.4mg of cholestrol
  • 162.4mg of sodium
